src.send_email.send_mail(message, subject)[source]

Sends an email to all guests from the wedding with the specified subject and message. The email will be sent from the “mariageseatingco@gmail.com” account.

Parameters

  • message (StringField) – The body of the email that has to be sent

  • subject (StringField) – The subject of the email that has to be sent

  • communication_list (List) – The mailing list. The list of email adress to which the email will be sent
